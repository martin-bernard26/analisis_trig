Invers Fungsi Trigonometri
\[\csc{\alpha}=\frac{1}{\sin{\alpha}}\]
\[\sec{\alpha}=\frac{1}{\cos{\alpha}}\]
\[\cot{\alpha}=\frac{1}{\tan{\alpha}}\]
Rumus Identitas
Rumus Identitas:\[\sin^{2}{\alpha}+\cos^{2}{\alpha}=1\]
Buktikan Rumus: \[\tan^{2}{\alpha}+1=\sec^{2}{\alpha}\]
Buktikan Rumus: \[\cot^{2}{\alpha}+1=\csc^{2}{\alpha}\]
